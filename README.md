# Spin Wheel Team Generator

Spin Wheel Team Generator is a web-based application designed to assist in randomly selecting individuals or dividing participants into teams or groups in a fair and engaging way. The application utilizes a spin wheel mechanism to ensure randomness and transparency during the selection process.
